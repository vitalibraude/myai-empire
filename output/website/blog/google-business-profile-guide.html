Google Business Profile: Complete Setup Guide for UK Businesses 2025

Published April 16, 2026 · by myAI · 12 min read

Why Google Business Profile Is the #1 Free Marketing Tool

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is the single most important free marketing tool for any local business. When someone searches "dentist near me" or "plumber in Manchester", the top results aren't websites — they're Google Business Profile listings.

Businesses with complete, optimised GBP listings get 7x more clicks and 70% more in-store visits than businesses without one. And it's completely free.

Step 1: Create Your Profile

Getting Started

Go to business.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click "Add your business to Google" and follow the prompts.

You'll need to provide:

  • Business name (exactly as it appears on your signage)
  • Business category (choose the most specific option available)
  • Business address or service area
  • Phone number
  • Website URL

Important: Use your real business name. Don't stuff keywords into your business name — Google will suspend your listing.

Step 2: Verify Your Business

Verification Methods

Google needs to verify you're a real business. Most businesses receive a postcard to their business address with a 5-digit code. Enter this code in your GBP dashboard.

Alternative verification methods (not available for all businesses):

  • Phone: Receive a verification code via automated call or text
  • Email: Get a code sent to your business email
  • Video: Record a short video showing your business location and signage

Step 3: Complete Your Profile 100%

Google rewards complete profiles with higher rankings. Fill in absolutely everything:

  • Hours: Regular hours AND special hours for holidays
  • Description: 750 characters describing your business (include keywords naturally)
  • Services/Products: List every service with descriptions and prices
  • Attributes: Mark all that apply (wheelchair accessible, free Wi-Fi, etc.)
  • Opening date: When your business started

Step 4: Add Photos (This Is Critical)

Businesses with photos get 42% more requests for directions and 35% more website clicks. Add at least 10 photos:

  • Your shopfront/exterior (helps Google Maps recognise your location)
  • Interior photos (show the environment)
  • Team photos (builds trust)
  • Product/service photos (show what you do)
  • Your logo (appears in search results)
  • Cover photo (displayed prominently on your listing)

Pro tip: Add new photos weekly. Businesses that regularly update photos rank higher.

Step 5: Get Reviews

Reviews are the #2 ranking factor for Google local results. Here's how to get more:

  1. Create a review link: In your GBP dashboard, find the "Get more reviews" card and copy the link
  2. Ask at the right time: Right after delivering great service
  3. Make it easy: Send the link via text message or email
  4. Respond to every review: Thank positive reviewers, address negative ones professionally
  5. Never buy fake reviews: Google detects them and will suspend your listing

Target: 10 reviews to look credible, 30+ to dominate your category locally, 100+ to be untouchable.

Step 6: Post Regularly

Google Business Profile has a built-in posting feature that most businesses ignore. Posts appear directly in your listing and signal to Google that your business is active.

Post types:

  • Updates: News, tips, or announcements
  • Offers: Promotions with start/end dates
  • Events: Upcoming events with dates and times
  • Products: Highlight specific products or services

Frequency: Aim for 1-2 posts per week. Each post stays visible for 7 days.

Step 7: Monitor and Optimise

Your GBP dashboard provides insights on:

  • How customers find your listing (search queries)
  • What actions they take (calls, website visits, direction requests)
  • Where they come from (geographic data)
  • Photo views compared to competitors

Review these monthly. If certain search terms bring traffic, create content around those terms on your website.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Keyword stuffing your business name: "John's Plumbing | Best Plumber London | Emergency Plumber" — Google will suspend you
  • Wrong category: If you're a "Dentist", don't choose "Dental Clinic" — be specific
  • Inconsistent NAP: Your Name, Address, and Phone must be identical everywhere online
  • Ignoring negative reviews: A thoughtful response to a negative review shows professionalism
  • Not updating hours: Customers who arrive when you're closed leave 1-star reviews

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Google Business Profile free?

Yes, Google Business Profile is completely free. Creating and maintaining your listing costs nothing. Google makes money from ads, not from business listings.

How long does it take to get verified?

Google typically sends a verification postcard within 5-14 days. Some businesses can verify by phone or email instantly. Video verification is also available for some categories.

How do I rank higher in Google Maps?

Key factors: complete your profile 100%, get more positive reviews (aim for 30+), add posts weekly, ensure NAP consistency across the web, choose the right categories, and keep your hours updated.
