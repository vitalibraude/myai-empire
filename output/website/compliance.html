Common questions.

The honest answers. No marketing fluff.

Does this actually replace a lawyer?
For the 90% of compliance work that is repetitive and procedural — yes. For novel legal questions (a data breach investigation, a specific EU member state's rules on biometric data), you still want a specialist. Aegis is your 24/7 compliance officer, not your trial attorney.
What data do you see?
Read-only metadata: file names, user permissions, cloud configuration, vendor list. Aegis does not read the content of your documents unless you explicitly enable deeper scanning, and even then, analysis runs in an isolated environment.
Does this cover Israel's new Privacy Protection Law amendment?
Yes. Amendment 13 aligned Israel's framework with GDPR-level requirements, and Aegis covers the full obligation set — DPO appointment guidance, breach notification windows, DPIAs, and the new enforcement powers of the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority.
We're pre-SOC2. Can Aegis get us there?
Aegis gets you to Type I (point-in-time readiness) without a consultant. For the Type II audit (continuous, 6-12 month observation), Aegis maintains the evidence locker your auditor requires. We can also introduce you to auditors we trust if you need a referral.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts. You can export all your policies and evidence when you leave. But fair warning — every enterprise customer you onboarded using the Trust Page link will get a 404 the day you cancel.
