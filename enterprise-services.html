FAQ

How long does SAP Business One implementation take?

A typical SAP Business One implementation for a mid-size manufacturer takes 8-16 weeks, depending on complexity. We follow SAP's Accelerated Implementation methodology.

What is Priority ERP and who is it for?

Priority ERP is a flexible, cloud-ready enterprise resource planning system for manufacturers, distributors, and service companies. It covers finance, supply chain, manufacturing, HR, CRM, and BI.

How much does Microsoft 365 implementation cost?

Microsoft 365 licensing starts from £4.60/user/month. Implementation and migration services typically cost £2,000-£15,000 depending on user count and complexity.

Can you migrate our data from legacy systems?

Yes. We specialise in data migration from legacy ERP systems, spreadsheets, and on-premise servers. We handle mapping, cleansing, validation, and parallel running.

Do you provide training for staff?

Every implementation includes structured training: admin training, super-user workshops, end-user sessions, and ongoing support documentation.
