The #1 Mistake UK Businesses Make on Google (It's Not What You Think)

April 17, 2026 · 8 min read · By myAI Team

I'm going to save you hundreds of hours and potentially thousands of pounds in one sentence:

If you haven't properly set up your Google Business Profile, nothing else you do for SEO matters.

56%

of UK local businesses haven't claimed or optimised their Google Business Profile

What This Costs You

When someone Googles "dentist near me" or "plumber in Manchester," Google shows three results at the top in a map box — the Local Pack. These three slots get 44% of all clicks.

If you're not in those three spots, you're invisible to nearly half your potential customers. And the #1 factor Google uses to choose those three spots? Your Google Business Profile.

Not your website. Not your ads. Not your social media. Your free, 10-minute-to-set-up Google Business Profile.

The 5 Sins We See in Every Audit

  1. Unclaimed profile — anyone can edit your info, and Google doesn't trust it.
  2. Wrong category — "Professional Services" instead of "Emergency Plumber." Google can't match you to the right searches.
  3. No photos — Listings with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more website clicks.
  4. No reviews / no replies — Businesses with 10+ reviews rank 15-20% higher than those with zero.
  5. Incomplete info — Missing hours, no service descriptions, no service area = Google ranks you lower.

The 10-Minute Fix

Minute 1-2: Claim Your Profile

Go to business.google.com. Search for your business. If it exists, claim it. If not, create it. You'll need to verify by postcard (3-5 days) or sometimes by phone.

Minute 3-4: Set the Right Category

Choose your primary category carefully — it's the biggest ranking factor. Be specific: "Cosmetic Dentist" beats "Dentist." Add 2-3 secondary categories.

Minute 5-6: Complete Every Field

Business hours (including bank holidays), phone number, website, service area, appointment link, description (750 chars — use keywords naturally).

Minute 7-8: Add 10+ Photos

Exterior, interior, team, your work (before/after). Update monthly. Businesses with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than average.

Minute 9-10: Ask for 5 Reviews

Text your 5 most recent happy customers now. Send them a direct link (from your GBP dashboard). 5 reviews is the minimum for the star rating to appear in search.

What Happens Next

Within 2-4 weeks of a properly optimised GBP, most businesses see:

  • 2-3x increase in Google Maps views
  • 40-60% more phone calls from search
  • 25-35% more website visits from Google

All free. All from a 10-minute setup.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Google Business Profile?

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a free listing that shows your business in Google Maps and local search results. It displays your name, address, phone, hours, reviews, and photos. It's the #1 factor for appearing in the Local Pack.

How much does Google Business Profile cost?

Google Business Profile is completely free. It costs nothing to set up, claim, and maintain.

How long does it take to set up?

Initial setup takes 10-15 minutes. Google verification takes 5-14 days. Full optimisation with photos and posts takes about 1-2 hours total.
