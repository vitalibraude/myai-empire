How to Choose the Right Tools

  1. Start with your biggest time waster. What takes the most hours each week? Start there.
  2. Try free tiers first. Most AI tools offer enough for free to evaluate them properly.
  3. Don't use more than 5 tools. Tool overload is real. Pick 3-5 core tools and master them.
  4. Check GDPR compliance. For UK businesses, ensure tools have proper data processing agreements.
  5. Measure ROI. Track time saved and revenue generated. If a tool doesn't save you at least 2x its cost, drop it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What AI tools should every small business use?

Every small business should use: ChatGPT or Claude for writing, Canva AI for design, Google Business Profile for local SEO, a chatbot for customer service, and an AI email tool for marketing.

Are AI tools safe to use for business?

Most reputable AI tools are safe. Check their privacy policy, avoid sharing sensitive customer data, use business accounts with proper security, and ensure GDPR compliance for UK businesses.

How much do AI tools cost?

Many AI tools have free tiers sufficient for small businesses. Paid plans typically range from £10-50/month. The average small business spends £50-150/month on AI tools total.
